The thesis

Most trading agents are too eager.

NullTrade AI is optimized for trade filtering, not signal generation. It assumes the incoming strategy may have useful ideas, but only some deserve execution after liquidity, spread, funding, volatility, recent performance, and news regime are checked.

The edge is refusal. A rejected trade is not an empty output; it is a capital-preserving decision with a verifiable reason attached.

Safety model

Live trading is possible, but highly guarded.

Users connect their own Bitget key. The app encrypts it, applies an order cap, restricts symbols, asks for confirmation, and only submits when the agent says TRADE.
